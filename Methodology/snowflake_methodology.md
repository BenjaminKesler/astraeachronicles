# Astraea Chronicles: Custom Snowflake Methodology

A custom iterative development methodology for the Astraea Chronicles novel series. Inspired by Randy Ingermanson's Snowflake Method, adapted for: an episodic multi-protagonist conspiracy thriller series, a human-AI collaboration model, and an ongoing craft theory ingestion process.

**Current status:** Skeleton only. Full revalidation scheduled after craft video queue is cleared.

---

## Terminology

- **Step** — one domain of development (e.g., Premise, Characters, Series Arc)
- **Pass** — one full loop through all steps active in the current stage, returning to Step 1 each time
- **Stage** — a level defined by how many steps are included in a pass; each stage adds one new step to the end

---

## Phase 1: Development Loop

Fully iterative. Each stage adds one new step to the pass sequence. Within a stage, multiple passes may be run before advancing to the next stage. The loop always begins from Step 1 (Premise) regardless of current stage — ensuring all earlier domains are re-evaluated as new ones are added.

1. Premise
2. Characters
3. Series Arc
4. World

**Note on ordering:** Characters precedes Series Arc intentionally — character psychology shapes the series arc, not the reverse. This is a deliberate departure from most outlining frameworks and consistent with the project's character-first design philosophy.
