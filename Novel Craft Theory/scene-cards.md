FOUR-SLOT FRAMEWORK (Lisa Cron, Story Genius)
  per scene, four required elements + one connector:
  PLOT LAYER:    what happens + consequence
  INTERNAL LAYER: why it matters + realization
  CONNECTOR:     "and so..." → cause-effect link to next scene
  both layers required in every scene — neither is optional
  for plot/internal dual-track theory: → [[internal-conflict]], [[plot-structure-outlining]]

REALIZATION (scene-level micro-shift)
  distinct from macro aha moment — smaller; a crack, not a dissolution
  character registers something shifted without fully understanding what
  accumulates across scenes toward macro transformation
  diagnostic: what does my character walk away from this scene knowing or feeling differently?

"AND SO..." CONNECTOR (cause-effect diagnostic)
  complete the sentence: "[what happened], and so..."
  if you can't complete it → scene is episodic (events happen), not causal (events cause each other)
  episodic scenes can be cut without structural loss; causal scenes cannot
