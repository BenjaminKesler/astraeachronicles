WOUND LAYER
state: misbelief operative under new plan at peak intensity; overcompensation at maximum; protagonist proactive; dramatic irony at highest tragic quality; protagonist most certain = reader most dreading; self-causer pattern accumulating; abstract desire subterranean

  THE NEW IMPOSSIBLE CHOICE (structural parallel to Build-Up)
    GCM disrupted the original plan and forced a new orientation — this phase is the protagonist's response
    same pain-vs-pain structure as Build-Up's impossible choice; both sides loaded heavier by accumulated Act 2A investment
    "while still avoiding fear" still the operative assumption: new plan believed to deliver desire AND sidestep wound
    → [[Beats/03_build_up]] impossible choice structure (original); [[Beats/04_first_plot_point]] "while still avoiding fear"

  MISBELIEF-AS-DECISION-ENGINE (non-negotiable — carries forward)
    every decision traceable to protagonist's specific misbelief; same requirement as pre-midpoint phase
    now executing at overcompensation peak: protagonist most convicted, most active, most certain
    choices must look rational from inside the misbelief AND externally reasonable to an outside observer
    CRITICAL DISTINCTION: the false frame is the only thing making them wrong — not incompetence
    "competent choices through false frame": reader sees the logic; the frame is what's wrong; dread not frustration
    → [[universal-diagnostics]] character lens audit; [[plot-structure-outlining]] misbelief-steered plan

  PROGRESSIVE COMPLEXITY (McKee — distinct from escalation)
    escalation ≠ stakes higher; escalation = dimensional complexity increasing
    each complication opens new dimensions of the problem that can't be undone; situation grows more entangled, not just harder
    prior events retroactively re-keyed by new information; the shape of the problem changes, not only its magnitude
    CONSPIRACY THRILLER APPLICATION
      each real win expands the conspiracy map rather than shrinking it; protagonist interprets expansion as progress
      misbelief form in thriller: "more information = closer to safety / answer / exit" — finish line keeps moving
      each discovery adds a new dimension AND retroactively re-keys what came before
      protagonist's real competence aimed through a false frame = tragic competence; wins are real; framing is wrong
    → [[Beats/07_game_changing_midpoint]] retroactive re-examination (mechanism continues through Act 2B)

  MICRO-CYCLE ARCHITECTURE (continues from pre-midpoint)
    McKee gap + Weiland R→R→R structure continues; orientation shifts (reactive → proactive); architecture unchanged
    each cycle closes a wound-avoidance sub-route within the new plan; compression accelerating
    protagonist working hardest as options narrow; running toward a wall they can't see yet
    → [[Beats/06_pre_midpoint_reactionary_hero]] micro-cycle architecture (origin)

  TWO-TENSION-TRACK AT MAXIMUM
    both tracks at peak intensity simultaneously — the same proactive behavior drives both:
      track 1 (genre): protagonist doing the most genre thing with most conviction and competence
      track 2 (tragic irony): protagonist most certain of the misbelief precisely when most wrong
    genre satisfaction and misbelief escalation are the same behavior in Act 2B; no gap between them
    this convergence is what makes Act 2B feel like the meat and potatoes of the story
    → [[internal-conflict]] SURFACE LAYER (two tension tracks); [[wound-model-thesis]] Part 3

---

GENRE LAYER
state: genre validating (consistency check), not constitutive; genre experience at maximum intensity

  TRAGIC-GENRE-FUN CONVERGENCE (core Act 2B property)
    protagonist's proactive overcompensation IS the genre delivery — not genre at the expense of wound mechanics
    genre satisfaction and misbelief escalation are the same behavior; convergence, not competition
    "Promise of the Premise" (Snyder) runs at full force — protagonist delivering the genre experience the reader came for
    thriller application earns this convergence hardest: competence is the genre promise; tragic competence is the convergence
    → [[Beats/06_pre_midpoint_reactionary_hero]] genre layer (Promise of the Premise established there)

---

SYMPATHY LAYER
state: protagonist certainty IS reader dread; tragic irony at peak; reader traces the chain protagonist cannot see

  false victory entry: protagonist's conviction is the reader's dread; maximum misbelief commitment = maximum tragic irony
  false defeat entry: urgent crisis-driven doubling down; competence deployed under pressure; reader watches trajectory
  character lens requirement: if choices look dumb → identification breaks; must look reasonable from outside the misbelief
  → [[Beats/07_game_changing_midpoint]] sympathy layer (tragic quality first arrives there)
  → [[universal-diagnostics]] mirror neurons vs. identification

---

RELATIONAL LAYER
function: DEEPENING COST — relational bonds under progressive complexity pressure; accumulated cost compounding toward visibility

  relationships recruited into the new plan tested under increasing complexity
  progressive complexity creates new relational entanglements — each new dimension has relational implications
  found family context: wound-protective mechanism friction as primary relational texture
    each protagonist under maximum stress reverts to dominant mechanism; mechanism mismatches generate friction
    friction without fracture: overcompensator dominates when others need space; avoider retreats when presence needed
    friction is wounds expressing themselves under pressure — not betrayal; found family is being tested, not destroyed
    → [[wound-protective-mechanisms]] SCENE FRICTION (mechanism mismatch between characters)
  forward: relational cost loading here surfaces as specific pressure at Second PP
  → [[Beats/07_game_changing_midpoint]] relational layer (first visible cost = starting point here)

---

FAILURE MODES
  SWAPPABLE PLAN: new plan not traceable to protagonist's specific misbelief; any character executes same plan; identification collapses
  PUNCHING BAG: progressive complexity overwhelms protagonist's agency; protagonist absorbs threats without executing own decisions; pity replaces identification
  FALSE CLARITY: plan looks dumb rather than competent-through-false-frame; reader judges not dreads; character lens audit failure
  FLAT COMPLEXITY: new dimensions feel additive (more problems) not entangling (interconnected, can't be undone); escalation stalls
  AHA MOMENT ENCROACHMENT: progressive complexity strips the false frame prematurely; abstract desire approaches legibility; disaster must do this work

---

DESIGN REQUIREMENTS
  character lens audit: new plan rational inside misbelief AND externally reasonable — false frame is the only thing making it wrong
  progressive complexity: each complication opens new dimensions + entangles prior events; not only intensity increase
  micro-cycle continuity: McKee gap + Weiland R→R→R structure unchanged; urgency and compression increase; proactive orientation doesn't change the architecture
  genre delivery: tragic-genre-fun convergence non-negotiable; both tension tracks at full force simultaneously
  aha moment protection: no approach toward abstract desire legibility; revelation belongs to disaster and dark moment

  SEEDING OBLIGATION (Act 2B extension of pre-midpoint principle)
    Act 2B scenes carry double-coded meaning for the disaster's self-causer mechanism
    design each competent-looking choice for what it will mean when the disaster reveals the full causal chain
    reader looking back from the disaster should be able to trace each Act 2B decision to the protagonist's position
    parallel to pre-midpoint seeding for GCM retroactive re-key; this version seeds the disaster's self-causer reveal
    → [[Beats/06_pre_midpoint_reactionary_hero]] seeding obligation (GCM-focused; this extends the principle to disaster)

  POSITION INHERITANCE
    from GCM: overcompensation at peak + misbelief intensification = opening state; charge polarity colors entry texture (false victory → maximum confidence; false defeat → crisis urgency); Doorway #2 committed; first visible cost = relational starting point; first protagonist state shift in Act 2 — this phase is first execution from new state
    from Pre-Midpoint Reactionary Hero: phase-end exhaustion = transition mechanism; micro-cycle architecture carries forward at higher urgency; relational resources now under higher complexity pressure; seeding obligation extends into Act 2B
    from First PP: wound-calibrated antagonist at closer range; Act 2A internal pinches closed sub-routes; reader information advantage at maximum
    from FPP: "while still avoiding fear" still operative for new plan; values revelation still subterranean; commitment cost compounding
    from Build-Up: impossible choice structure replays at higher compression; accumulated Act 2A investment loads both sides heavier than original Build-Up
    from II: crystallized desire = specific goal being pursued through new plan; story question still unanswered
    from Hook: abstract desire incongruity leak still subterranean; mechanism at full strength baseline = floor the arc has climbed from
    what this phase opens: Second PP — antagonist has gained ground from protagonist's prior choices; felt enclosure begins; relational bonds specifically pressured; disaster approaching
      → [[Beats/09_second_pinch_point]]

DIAGNOSTIC QUESTIONS
  does the new plan look rational from inside the misbelief AND externally reasonable — is the false frame the only thing making it wrong? (character lens audit)
  can every decision be traced to the protagonist's specific misbelief? (swappability test)
  is each micro-cycle escalating in dimensional complexity (new entanglements opening up) — not only in intensity (same problem harder)? (progressive complexity check)
  is the protagonist visibly executing a plan — not being acted upon by complications? (pity trap / punching bag check)
  are both tension tracks running at full force — genre satisfaction AND tragic irony simultaneously? (two-tension-track check)
  are Act 2B scenes designed with the disaster's self-causer mechanism in mind? (seeding obligation check)
  is abstract desire remaining subterranean — no approach toward aha moment territory? (aha moment protection check)
