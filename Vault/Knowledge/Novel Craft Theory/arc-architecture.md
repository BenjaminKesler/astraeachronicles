CONVENTIONS
  two orthogonal axes define every arc; the named arcs = inhabited points in their space
  int = internal outcome (arc direction)  |  ext = external outcome  |  → cross-ref
  SYNONYMS (standardized — same referent, not distinct concepts):
    "misbelief" = canonical; "the lie" = the same thing (kept here only in the named-arc shorthand)
    "positive arc" = internal WIN = misbelief dissolved; "negative arc" = internal LOSS = misbelief embraced (Axis B)
    → [[universal-diagnostics]] TERM AXES (internal/external outcome)

---

AXIS A — ARC ARCHITECTURE  (whole-arc commitment, NOT a late fork; shapes beats from the Hook)
  STRIPPING ("stripped of the lie") — exogenous: accumulated self-caused catastrophe BREAKS the misbelief
    Disaster → Dark Moment → Aha   → [[Beats/10_disaster_dark_moment]]
  TEMPTATION ("found something truer") — endogenous: abstract desire grows until the misbelief is voluntarily released
    Temptation → Aha; reshapes Hook (incongruity leak planted harder), GCM (Doorway #2 toward desire), Act 2B (desire surfaces)
    → [[Beats/10b_temptation]]
  selection: does transformation arrive by catastrophic stripping, or by finding something genuinely better?

AXIS B — ARC CONCLUSION  (Stripping only; 2×2 = internal × external outcome)
  internal: WIN (misbelief dissolved) vs LOSS (misbelief embraced as truth — negative arc → [[plot-structure-outlining]])
  external: WIN vs LOSS

---

THE FIVE ARCS  (named by reader emotional register; sympathetic-protagonist default)

                  ext-WIN          ext-LOSS
   int-WIN     VINDICATION          ELEGY        ← soul intact
   int-LOSS     DAMNATION          TRAGEDY       ← soul lost
                       (Stripping architecture)

  VINDICATION  transformation proven real; the world confirms the change
  ELEGY        mourning sung from wholeness; transformation sufficient despite external loss
  DAMNATION    wins the world, loses the self; moral horror watched in motion
  TRAGEDY      the preventable fall; the flaw destroys them and takes the world down with it
  SERENITY     [Temptation — off-grid] grew past the want and set it down; quiet, warm, wistful; no floor

WHY FIVE (not 8): Temptation forces int-WIN and dissolves the ext axis → its quadrant collapses to one arc;
  negative-arc Temptation is incoherent (you cannot refuse the goal-object because you grew worse)

---

SYMPATHY-INVERSION MODIFIER  (reader alignment; attaches ONLY to the int-LOSS row)
  reader aligned AGAINST the protagonist re-colors the register:
    DAMNATION → INJUSTICE  (the bad actor takes the prize)
    TRAGEDY   → JUSTICE    (the bad actor is destroyed)
  int-WIN arcs cannot flip — rooting for a transformation bakes sympathy in
  NOT a third axis (would double the cube): a conditional tag on two points only

DIVERGENCE MAP  (where each axis bites the construction — for recall / re-keying an arc)
  ARCHITECTURE   → from the Hook onward (whole-arc build)
  INTERNAL outcome → at the AHA (dissolve the lie vs commit to it)   → [[Beats/11_aha_moment]]
  EXTERNAL outcome → at CLIMAX / VICTORY (2×2 quadrant + register settle)  → [[Beats/12_climactic_confrontation]], [[Beats/13_victory]]
  use: to re-key an arc, change only the beats the altered axis touches
