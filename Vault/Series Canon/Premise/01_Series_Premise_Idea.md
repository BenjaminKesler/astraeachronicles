## Raw Series Story Idea

Six of the galaxy's discarded — a defected golden-boy officer, an exiled royal in mourning, a pirate who arms his own refugees, a spy who traded her body for cybernetics, a half-breed hiding inside his own skin, and a refugee who jokes so he never has to look at the dark — find themselves crewing the *Astraea*: a prototype stealth ship they don't yet know is the enemy's master key.

They think they're investigating a thousand-year war between the human-Vuquoi Alliance and a monstrous third race, the Ka'ar. They're wrong about all of it. The Ka'ar don't exist. The war is a Vuquoi civil war, each side spoofed in real time to see the other as a monster. The Tal'Sha crown they'd blame has dirty hands — but it's a puppet too, as is the Syndicate beneath it, and the Ehmeya beneath them who believe they run a shadow empire. At the bottom is a species no one can quite perceive: the Aeliria, who have kept the war alive for a millennium by erasing perception itself — deleting truth before a mind can hold it.

Truth can't be broadcast; anything digital gets erased. So the crew steals it one un-hackable piece at a time — paper, intuition, analog — assembling an infrastructure of truth they can detonate all at once. They can't win loudly, and they can't win early. But if they can smuggle enough truth past a system built to delete it, the manufactured war collapses, the Aeliria's grip breaks, and the galaxy is finally released to see what's real.
