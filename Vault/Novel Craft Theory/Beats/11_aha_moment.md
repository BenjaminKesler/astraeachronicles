WOUND LAYER
state: misbelief recognized and dissolved (cognitive amplification structure gone — not the fear itself); abstract desire fully legible; wound-constructed identity seen through; protagonist chooses to act through remaining fear; internal war won; first truth-driven decision possible

  THREE-SHIFT STRUCTURE (the mechanism)
    COGNITIVE: misbelief recognized as false; cognitive worldview that amplified and managed the fear dissolves
      not fear elimination — the amplification/management structure that made fear controlling is what goes
    EPISTEMIC: abstract desire fully legible for the first time — "the good worth fighting for" finally visible
      always co-produced with fear at wound formation; misbelief was obscuring it, not replacing it
      abstract desire chain completes: Hook (incongruity leak) → II/Build-up (subterranean) → FPP (opaque) → Disaster (obstruction stripped) → Aha (fully legible)
      → [[Beats/01_hook]] Hauge nuance (chain origin); [[wound-model-thesis]] Part 4
    VOLITIONAL: protagonist chooses to act through remaining fear — courage as act of will, not absence of fear
      old story (misbelief) required fear to be managed; new story holds fear AND action simultaneously
      forming the new story IS the act of courage — cognitive restructuring and bravery are the same event (Cron)
      bravery = acting for something recognized as worth having in spite of fear that remains

  IDENTITY LAYER (Hauge, Vogler — deeper register of same dissolution)
    misbelief operates at two registers: cognitive (false rule: "this is how the world works") + identity (false self: "this is who I am")
    aha unwinds both simultaneously: not only "my rule was wrong" but "I am not who the wound told me I was"
    true identity pre-existed the wound; aha reveals it, doesn't construct it
    synthesis not conversion (Vogler): protagonist becomes more fully themselves — not a different person

  SELF-REVELATION REQUIREMENT (Truby)
    revelation must be about the self — not strategic insight about the external problem
    TWO REQUIRED LAYERS (both needed for complete arc):
      PSYCHOLOGICAL: "I now understand myself differently"
      MORAL: "I now know what is right and will live differently"
    psychological alone = incomplete arc; moral alone without psychological = didactic; both = transformation

  NEGATIVE ARC VARIANT
    aha still occurs; content inverts — protagonist forms new story that the misbelief IS the truth
    active commitment to the lie rather than failing to escape it
    author's truth transmitted through tragic negation: what was worth fighting for, refused
    → [[plot-structure-outlining]] negative arc (mechanics)

AUTHOR LAYER (unique to this beat — design criterion for content selection)
  "the rest of your story is packaging for this moment" — everything else is delivery architecture
  narrative's unique capacity: emotional transmission of truth that analytical discussion cannot replicate
    the aha moment is the thinnest wall between author and reader — not broken, but emotional pull crosses it
    this is the beat where the author transmits their truth, not a truth they constructed for the plot
  AUTHOR-TRUTH SELECTION CRITERION (before designing the beat, answer for yourself):
    "what is the good worth fighting for in this story?"
    this IS the content of the abstract desire at thematic register
    ONE PERSON DIAGNOSTIC: aim at the one person with this protagonist's exact wound, fears, misbelief — what would you tell them?
  TWO-TIER FAILURE DIAGNOSTIC:
    if beat reads as fuzzy or generic → ask the author-level question FIRST
      author hasn't found their truth → beat will be generic regardless of craft execution
      upstream delivery failed → beat has the right truth but runway wasn't built
    always diagnose author clarity before diagnosing craft failure

GENRE LAYER
state: genre validating (consistency check), not constitutive

  FIRST POSITIVE VALUE SWING IN ACT 3 (McKee)
    Disaster/Dark Moment held at global value floor — most negative charge in the story
    Aha = first positive charge shift in Act 3; structural reversal that makes the Climax possible
    without this shift: Climax has no internal foundation; external action cannot prove a transformation that hasn't happened
  → [[Beats/10_disaster_dark_moment]] value charge shift (floor established and held through Dark Moment)

---

SYMPATHY LAYER
state: tragic dread converts to vindication/hope; reader watches protagonist finally see what reader has held since the Hook

  reader has held abstract desire in view since Hook's incongruity leak — watching protagonist pursue wrong solutions
  aha = reader sees protagonist see what reader already knew; information gap closes from the inside
  sympathy state shift: dread → cheering/vindication — qualitatively distinct from all prior beats' sympathy states
  not relief but vindication — reader's long-held understanding confirmed by the character's own arrival
  identification reaches its most direct form: no wound-driven behavior left to see through; character and reader finally aligned
  → [[Beats/01_hook]] Hauge nuance (abstract desire leak — what reader held from there completes here)

---

RELATIONAL LAYER
function: RECONCILIATION/OPENING — protagonist can see wound-driven relational distortions clearly AND choose genuine engagement for first time

  distinct from Disaster's MORAL RECKONING:
    MORAL RECKONING (Disaster): "I see what my wound behavior cost others" — backward-facing
    RECONCILIATION/OPENING (Aha): "genuine connection is now possible; repair can begin" — forward-facing
  wound-protective relational patterns recognized as wrong solutions; character can now engage without routing through the mechanism
  relational repair doesn't complete here — it opens at Aha, executes through Climax and Resolution
  → [[Beats/10_disaster_dark_moment]] relational layer (moral reckoning this beat follows from)

  CALIBRATION
    subtext principle may relax slightly — one of the few beats where internal shift can surface more directly
    author's truth should still be FELT not stated; externalizing the aha into dialogue risks didacticism
    external catalyst (another character enabling the realization) is permitted — the realization itself must be internal
    Sam's speech is the rare exception, earned by a thousand pages of darkness; default to internal realization

---

FAILURE MODES
  DEUS EX MACHINA: another character delivers the truth; aha is externally received, not internally arrived at
    external catalyst ≠ external delivery; catalyst creates conditions, doesn't substitute for the internal event
    diagnostic: could the protagonist have arrived here alone, given enough dark moment time? if no → deus ex machina
    if deus ex machina is tempting → upstream abstract desire seeding is likely insufficient; look there first
  SURPRISE DEPLETED (Cron): protagonist has been consciously working toward the insight; arrives as conclusion not realization
    protagonist's own pursuit can self-deplete the aha — same structural damage as external theft
  AHA MOMENT THEFT: upstream beat surfaced abstract desire explicitly; nothing left to dissolve into
    → [[Beats/10_disaster_dark_moment]] failure modes (defined and prevention designed there)
  PSYCHOLOGICAL ONLY: protagonist understands themselves differently but moral layer absent; arc incomplete (Truby)
  EMOTIONAL UPLIFT ONLY: protagonist feels hope or relief without cognitive restructuring; new story never forms; courage has no foundation
  GENERIC TRUTH: aha could belong to any protagonist; not calibrated to this specific wound
    fix direction: return to author-truth layer; find the specific good worth fighting for this wound configuration
  PREMATURE AHA: dark moment hasn't fully stripped the misbelief; revelation arrives before conditions are earned
    → [[Beats/10_disaster_dark_moment]] dark moment quality check (forced confrontation requirement)

---

DESIGN REQUIREMENTS
  AUTHOR-TRUTH FIRST: identify the truth before designing the delivery; craft cannot compensate for author-level unclarity
  INTERNAL SOURCE: realization must come from within; external catalysts enable, don't substitute
  THREE SHIFTS REQUIRED: cognitive + epistemic + volitional; any one alone is insufficient for complete transformation
  BOTH SELF-REVELATION LAYERS: psychological and moral (Truby); design explicitly for both
  SCENE-EARNING CONNECTION: aha is a major emotional payoff; planted resonance mechanic must have been running upstream
    protagonist's micro-contact with abstract desire across Acts 1–2 = emotional substrate the aha fires from
    if payoff reads as intellectual not felt → look upstream for planted resonance deficit, not inside the aha scene
    → [[scene-earning]] reverse-engineer protocol; distribution principle

  POSITION INHERITANCE
    from Dark Moment: mechanism stripped; misbelief-as-refuge gone; protagonist in most unfamiliar state; abstract desire obstruction removed but not yet legible; three-beat unit ready for resolution; relational moral reckoning begun
    from Disaster: self-causer chain complete; kryptonite exposed; both fear layers hit simultaneously; "while still avoiding fear" finally and completely disproved; comfort zone loop closing
    from FPP: abstract desire chain (opaque values revelation → fully legible here); "while still avoiding fear" — committed there, disproved at Disaster, transcended here
    from Hook: abstract desire incongruity leak (Hauge) — chain origin; comfort zone made real at Hook, violated at Disaster, echoed at Aha (protagonist sees what they were always reaching for)
    what this beat opens: Climax — first action taken from dissolved-misbelief state under external pressure; internal war already won; Climax = external proof that the transformation holds

DIAGNOSTIC QUESTIONS
  what is my truth — the good worth fighting for in this story? (author-truth check)
  who is the one person with this protagonist's exact wound — what would I tell them? (one person targeting check)
  does the realization come from within the protagonist, or does another character deliver it? (deus ex machina check)
  does it arrive as realization or conclusion — was the protagonist consciously pursuing this insight? (surprise check)
  does the revelation address the self, not just the external situation? (self-revelation check)
  are both psychological and moral layers present — "I understand myself differently" AND "I will live differently"? (Truby check)
  does cognitive restructuring produce a new story to act from — or only a feeling? (Cron check)
  what is the "good worth fighting for" — abstract desire in thematic register? (epistemic content check)
  has enough abstract desire micro-contact been planted upstream for the realization to come from within? (seeding / deus ex machina prevention)
  is the global value charge shifting positively for the first time in Act 3? (value floor check)
