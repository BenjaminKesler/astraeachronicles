🃏 Roan: The Tragic Comedian

> _"Keep them laughing, keep the lights bright, and whatever you do, don't look out the window at the dark."_

- **Enneagram:** **Type 7 (The Enthusiast) with an 8 Wing**
- **The Overarching Theme Mirror:** He uses toxic positivity and frantic distraction to perform a personal erasure of the dark reality around him, mimicking the very apathy the Aeliria enforce on the galaxy.
- **Species:** Human
- **Backstory & History:** A civilian refugee who watched his home burn. His family was killed in the orbital strike Alistair commanded on what the UDI believed was a Ka'ar military stronghold — it was actually a Vuqtai civilian settlement, the target spoofed by the Aeliria. He escaped the trauma by detaching entirely, serving as Alistair's non-military civilian aide and using manic humor, fast-talking diplomacy, and desperate optimism to survive.
- **The Internal Lie / Misbelief:** _"If I stop joking, if I acknowledge the capability for violence within me, the pain will swallow me alive and I will become a monster."_
- **The Vulnerable Virtue (Why Readers Love Him):** He is the emotional heart and joy of the ship. His humor is genuinely funny and brings levity to the grim corridors of _The Astraea_. He deeply loves people and uses his wit to diffuse tension, acting as the only person on the ship who isn't afraid to call Alistair out when he acts like a pompous dictator.
- **The Core Conflict & Exploitation:** The Aeliria use his fear of pain to keep him entirely passive. By making violence look completely monstrous, they ensure he stays too afraid to ever pick up a weapon or interfere with their plans.
- **The Turning Point & Breaking the Illusion:** _(Note: Roan's family was killed in the orbital strike Alistair commanded. Neither knows the other is connected to it — the reveal is intended to land later in the story.)_ Roan is physically present with Karae when the Aeliria begin her active targeted erasure. The Aeliria's passive ambient erasure operates on him simultaneously — the automatic reflex they cannot suppress. The Aeliria are psychically connected to both at once; his human perceptual system, its boundaries compromised by the passive erasure, reaches toward the enormous void being created in Karae. His gap-filling response follows the Aeliria's own psychic focus toward their target and he falls through the gap they made, into the inner world her brain has constructed. Karae reaches for him and pulls him through — making him the first person she has ever actively let in. Inside, none of his tools work. He cannot navigate, cannot deflect, cannot perform. The space has no tolerance for armor. He cannot find her. He can only stop trying and be honest in the dark — confessing the thing he has never said aloud: watching his family burn while he cracked a manic joke to numb the panic. He speaks it without knowing if she can hear him, without audience, without protection. She lets him into the room not because he is strong enough to hold what is there, but because he is broken enough to understand it. _(The cost to Roan — what traveling through an active Aeliria erasure circuit permanently takes from him — is real and lasting per North Star canon. Specifics to be defined in his character analysis.)_
- **The Ultimate Epiphany:** Pain and dark choices cannot be avoided if you want to protect what you love. He stops running from reality and uses his sharp observation of human behavior to design the crew's main weapon: an analog strobe and paint-dispersal system that strips away the Aeliria's cloaking. Forcing the truth into the light initiates a bloody, close-quarters massacre of the exposed agents, forcing the man who wanted only joy to carry a heavy, dark truth to save the galaxy.
